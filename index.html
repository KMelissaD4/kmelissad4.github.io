About Me

Krista Delucchi is a Project and Program Manager with years of experience working for tech companies. Always seeking improvement, she is currently studying to sit for the Project Management Professional exam to obtain certification as a PMP, through the respected Project Management Institute (PMI). She plans to obtain additional certification from PMI as an Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP). Krista has also registered for a seminar and subsequent exam to become a Certified Scrum Master, via Mike Cohn's Mountain Goat Software organization. Lastly, she is working toward a completion certificate for a Coding Boot Camp from UC Berkeley's Extension School.

As a Project Manager, Krista has built a portfolio of successful projects, overhauling processes, tools, and engagements at each place of work. Her PM portfolio is displayed on her Confluence site, kdelucchi.atlassian.net. She hopes to create an extensive portfolio of web development projects and leverage her new skills to work as a Technical Project Manager for software teams.

Very recently, Krista became engaged to be married to her partner, Omar. Omar and Krista are in the process of closing on the purchase of a house in Santa Cruz, CA. She has 2 beautiful kitties, Jenny, aged 10 and Penelope, aged 6 months.
